My solution:
The Truth Model

A strict 4-layer model that separates what a contract says from what it means, what can be derived, and what someone thinks.

📌

FACT

Directly grounded in contract text. Immutable.

"Term: 24 months"
🔗

BINDING

Explicit semantic mapping. Scoped.

"Company" = Acme Corp
💡

INFERENCE

Derived claim with confidence. Revisable.

Expires Dec 2025 (0.92)
⚖️

OPINION

Contextual judgment. Never persisted as truth.

Missing force majeure = risk

Every answer is auditable. Every claim is traceable to source text.

Three-Phase
Extraction Pipeline

Upload a PDF or DOCX. Get structured intelligence in seconds.

1

Pattern Extraction

Regex + structural parsing. Definitions, dates, amounts, durations, table data. Zero LLM calls.

2

Semantic Indexing

FAISS + MiniLM-L6-v2 (384-dim vectors). Every fact, clause, and binding becomes searchable by meaning.

3

LLM Discovery

Anthropic Claude finds what patterns miss — hidden obligations, implicit risks, cross-clause implications.

Phases 1 & 2 are deterministic and reproducible.
Phase 3 is AI-augmented — it finds what patterns miss.
