I built an AI Operating System for Contracts — and it's open source.

For my Capstone project, I didn't build another document chatbot.

I built ContractOS — a system that transforms static legal documents into structured, queryable, explainable legal knowledge.

Here's what makes it different:

𝗧𝗵𝗲 𝗧𝗿𝘂𝘁𝗵 𝗠𝗼𝗱𝗲𝗹
Most contract AI treats everything the same. ContractOS separates:
→ FACT — what the contract says (immutable)
→ BINDING — what terms mean (scoped)
→ INFERENCE — what can be derived (confidence-scored)
→ OPINION — what someone thinks (never persisted as truth)

Every answer is auditable. Every claim is traceable to source text.

𝗧𝗵𝗿𝗲𝗲-𝗣𝗵𝗮𝘀𝗲 𝗣𝗶𝗽𝗲𝗹𝗶𝗻𝗲

2️⃣ Semantic Indexing — FAISS + MiniLM-L6-v2 (384-dim vectors)
3️⃣ LLM Discovery — Anthropic Claude finds what patterns miss
